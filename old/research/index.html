Ongoing research

Field: graph theory, bipartite matching

Stable Matching problem under Social stability




The question we are interested in analyzing is the following: Given a set of preference lists for all men and women, one per- son is in another persons realm of possible spouses if there is a stable matching in which the two people are married. A persons optimal spouse is their most pre- ferred person within their realm of possibility. A persons pessimal spouse is their least preferred person in their realm of possibility. TMA the matching algorithm marries every man to his optimal spouse. TMA marries every woman to her pessimal spouse.
